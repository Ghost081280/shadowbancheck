â“ Why Hashtags Get Banned

  • Spam abuse: Hashtags overused by spammers and bots
  • Inappropriate content: Associated with adult or harmful material
  • Community violations: Used to spread misinformation or hate speech
  • Temporary restrictions: Hashtags can be banned temporarily, then unbanned
  • Misuse: Even innocent hashtags like #desk or #beautyblogger can get flagged

ğŸ’¡ Best Practices

  • Always check hashtags before posting
  • Use 10-15 well-chosen hashtags per post (not the max 30)
  • Mix popular, niche, and branded hashtags
  • Avoid using ALL popular/generic hashtags
  • Research hashtags specific to your niche
  • Check regularly - ban status changes weekly
  • Don't repeat the same hashtag sets every post
