Frequently Asked Questions

What is a shadow ban?

A shadow ban is when a social media platform restricts the visibility of your posts or account to others, often without telling you. It's usually a result of violating community guidelines, even if unintentionally.

How accurate is the checker?

Our checker uses a combination of direct API checks (where available) and an AI model trained on millions of data points to assess 10+ factors affecting visibility, giving you a high-probability score (not a guarantee) in under 60 seconds.

Is my account information safe?

Yes. We only require public information (username or post URL) and do not ask for passwords or any private credentials. All communication is secured with SSL encryption.

Why do I get limited free checks?

Running real-time checks across 26 platforms requires significant server resources. We offer 3 free checks per day to ensure everyone can use the tool, but unlimited checks are reserved for our Pro subscribers.

What happens after the 7-day free trial?

If you choose not to cancel before the trial ends, your card will be automatically charged $119.88 for the annual subscription. You can easily manage or cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard.

Can Shadow AI help me recover?

Yes. Shadow AI Pro provides personalized recovery strategies based on your specific shadow ban profile, guiding you step-by-step to restore your visibility and reach on each platform.
