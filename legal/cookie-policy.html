Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ShadowBanCheck.io ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. We believe in transparency and want you to understand what data we collect and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience. We primarily use local storage, which is similar to cookies but stays on your device and is not sent to our servers with each request.

How We Use Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage for the following purposes:

Essential Functions

These are necessary for the website to function properly:

Name Purpose Duration
cookies_accepted Remembers that you've acknowledged our cookie notice Persistent

Functionality

These help us provide features and remember your preferences:

Name Purpose Duration
shadowAI_questions Tracks your daily AI assistant question usage (resets daily) 24 hours
searchCount Tracks free daily searches to enforce usage limits 24 hours
lastSearchDate Records the date of your last search for daily reset Persistent

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Payment Processors - When you subscribe to a paid plan, our payment provider (e.g., Stripe) may set cookies to process your transaction securely.
  • Analytics - We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

What We Don't Do

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We don't sell your data to third parties
  • We don't use tracking cookies for advertising
  • We don't store your social media passwords
  • We don't share your shadow ban check results with anyone

Managing Your Preferences

You have control over cookies and local storage:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies/local storage are set
  • Delete specific cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is set

Clearing Our Data

To clear data stored by ShadowBanCheck.io:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the "Application" tab
  3. Click "Local Storage" in the sidebar
  4. Right-click on our domain and select "Clear"

Note: Clearing this data will reset your free search count and cookie preferences.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies or local storage for our site:

  • The cookie consent banner will appear on every visit
  • Your free search count won't be tracked (you may get unlimited or zero free searches)
  • Shadow AI question limits won't persist between sessions

The core shadow ban checking functionality will still work.
