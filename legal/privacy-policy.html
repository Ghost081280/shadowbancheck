How We Perform Shadow Ban Checks

When you request a shadow ban check, we:

  1. Query publicly available information from social media platforms using their official APIs where available
  2. Analyze public profile accessibility, search visibility, and content reach
  3. Use our AI system to interpret signals and provide probability assessments
  4. Generate a report based on our analysis

Important: We only access publicly available information. We never log into your accounts or access private data. Your check results are for your eyes only.
